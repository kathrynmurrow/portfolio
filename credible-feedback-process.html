Instructional leadership framework

Credible feedback process

A four-step approach to observation, analysis, and accountable support.

01
Step one

Attain knowledge of the standards

  • PLC presence
  • See/hear charts
  • Vertical alignment of standards
  • Content-specific professional learning
02
Step two

Conduct classroom observations

  • Observation roles
  • Low-inference data
  • See/hear charts
  • Calibration
03
Step three

Analyze data and determine area of focus

  • Synthesize findings
  • Data-driven area of focus
  • Cumulative coaching evidence
  • Feedback trends
04
Step four

Deliver standards-based feedback

  • Actionable next steps
  • Evidence-aligned support
  • Accountability through follow-up
  • Coaching documentation
