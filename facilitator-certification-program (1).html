Bojangles · Training & Development

Facilitator Certification Program

Over the course of 6–9 months, participants attend three in-person sessions focused on planning, questioning, and facilitating using structured activities and learner-centered approaches.

3 Sessions
6–9 month program

Program Culmination

Culminating Presentation & Peer Review

The program culminates in a learner-developed presentation given to a room of peers, who provide feedback based on what they have learned throughout the program.

The Certification Journey
Session 01

Planning & Preparation

Building the foundation for effective facilitation. Participants learn how to structure sessions, set learning objectives, and prepare materials that drive engagement and retention.

Key Resources

Training Playbook Session Planning Guide Activity Design Templates
Session 02

Questioning & Engagement

Mastering learner-centered questioning techniques. Participants practice using open-ended questions with a specific focus, and have multiple opportunities to apply questioning with their peers before implementation.

Key Resources

Questioning Toolkit Planning Strategic Questions Questioning Practice
Session 03

Facilitation & Delivery

Putting it all together through practice facilitation. Participants deliver a learner-developed presentation to their peers and receive structured feedback on their approach.
