Facilitator Certification Program

Over the course of 6–9 months, participants attend three in-person sessions focused on planning, questioning, and facilitating using structured activities and learner-centered approaches.

3 Sessions
6–9 month program
Program culmination

Culminating Presentation & Peer Review

The program culminates in a learner-developed presentation delivered to peers, followed by structured feedback rooted in the practices introduced throughout the certification journey.

The Certification Journey

Session 01

Planning & Preparation

Building the foundation for effective facilitation. Participants learn how to structure sessions, set learning objectives, and prepare materials that drive engagement and retention.

Key resources
Training Playbook Session Planning Guide Activity Design Templates
Session 02

Questioning & Engagement

Mastering learner-centered questioning techniques. Participants practice open-ended questions, discussion structures, and facilitation moves that increase ownership and interaction.

Key resources
Questioning Toolkit Discussion Protocols Facilitation Practice Guide
Session 03

Facilitation & Feedback

Applying all components in live facilitation. Participants receive peer and facilitator feedback, refine their delivery, and demonstrate readiness through the culminating presentation.
