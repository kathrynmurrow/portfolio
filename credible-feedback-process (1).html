Instructional leadership framework

Credible feedback process

A four-step approach to observation, analysis, and accountable support

01
Step one

Attain knowledge of the standards

  • PLC presence
  • See/hear charts
  • Vertical alignment of standards
  • Content-specific professional learning
02
Step two

Conduct classroom observations

  • Observation roles
  • Low-inference data
  • See/hear charts
  • Calibration
03
Step three

Analyze data to determine area of focus & feedback

  • Synthesize findings
  • Data-driven area of focus
  • Cumulative coaching
  • Feedback generation
04
Step four

Deliver feedback with accountable support systems

  • Differentiated feedback delivery
  • Specific next steps
  • Accountability measures
  • Coaching support systems
