Restaurant Visit Protocol
Area Director responsibilities
1

Pre-visit planning

Review data, set objectives, and prepare materials before arriving onsite.

2

Manager's walk / day part

Observe operations alongside the manager on duty and anchor the visit in real work.

3

Observations, coaching & feedback

Document findings, ask strong questions, and coach in real time.

4

Training Care Conversation

Facilitate a structured dialogue on development priorities and needed support.

5

Accountability & follow-up

Set commitments, define next steps, and confirm the follow-up cadence.
